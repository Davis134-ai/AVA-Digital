Airtel Money Instructions

  1. Dial *185# on your Airtel line to open the Airtel Money menu.
  2. Choose the option for "Withdraw Cash".
  3. Enter the amount.
  4. Once completed, put the secret code given to you in the input below and click "Confirm Recharge".
  5. Recharge will immediately reflect in your account wallet.

MTN MoMo Instructions

  1. Please wait 2–3 minutes maximum for a USSD prompt to come on your SIM card.
  2. Once you receive the USSD prompt from MTN, please input your 5-digit PIN to complete the transaction.
  3. Once the transaction is completed, please go back to your profile page — the new balance will reflect in about 1 minute.
  4. If no USSD prompt comes after the progress bar is filled, please dial *165# to check for any pending transactions and then input the PIN on your SIM card.
