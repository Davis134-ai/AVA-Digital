MTN MoMo Instructions

  1. Click the "Confirm Recharge" button below.
  2. Wait 1–2 minutes for a USSD prompt to appear on your phone with the phone number used to register your AVA-Digital account.
  3. You'll receive a prompt asking you to enter your 5-digit MoMo PIN to confirm the withdrawal.
  4. Enter your PIN and the recharge will immediately reflect in your account wallet.

Airtel Money Instructions

  1. Click the "Confirm Recharge" button below.
  2. Follow the instructions on how to recharge that will be presented to you on the next page.
  3. Enter your secret code and the recharge will immediately reflect in your account wallet.

🌙 Late Night Payment?

If you're making a payment between 12 AM and 6 AM, please be aware that there may be delays in processing due to limited mobile money agent availability during these hours. We recommend making payments between 6 AM and 12 AM for the fastest service. If you do make a late night payment, rest assured it will be processed as soon as possible the next day.

Other Payment Options

Our mobile money payments are orchestrated by a network of mobile money agents fully registered with both MTN and Airtel to create ease of service to our users during payments and provide convenience without difficult technical payment portals.
